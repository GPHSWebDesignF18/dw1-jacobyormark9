Dave Carson

Dave Carson is one of the most famous graphic designers as well as he is well known in the design media industry.

He is best known for his amazing magazine design, and his use of a style called "experimental typography". He was also the art director for the magazine Ray Gun, in which he used lots of the typographic and layout approach for which what he is best known for.

Education

What's the best way to become a media designer?

Employers often require candidates that have an associate's or bachelor's degree in multimedia design, or a somewhat related field. Bachelor degree programs are available in multimedia design, computer science, graphic design, digital art and other related fields.

Salary

Is it worth it?

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, multimedia artists and animators, which also include digital media designers, earn an annual wage of around $63,970 and a hourly wage of $30.76 (www.bls.gov) in 2015.
